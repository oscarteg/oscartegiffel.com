I was a down-to-earth person I told myself. But saying that also blocked me off from any other wording that people had.
Talks about energy, vibration, confirmation I thought was bullshit. I needed those abstract words to exist in the materialistic words but there is more than the materialistic world. Emotions and feelings are real, and they shape our reality. The lies we tell ourselves can limit our potential and keep us from achieving our dreams.
