**AI code generation works much like a compiler producing assembly language.** Could a skilled human write better code? Probably. But for most everyday tasks, the output is good enough — and that's the point. Few developers write assembly by hand anymore. The interesting question isn't whether AI-generated code is optimal. It's whether it's fit for purpose. Most of the time, it is. That frees you to focus on the parts that actually require judgment.
